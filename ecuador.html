Galápagos Flight Day (Dec 11) — The Shuffle

⚠️ Strict Order — Do Not Skip or Reorder Steps
You cannot go straight to check-in or the lounge. Even with Business Class or Priority Pass, everyone waits in the same INGALA line. Add 30 extra minutes to your airport arrival time.
Step 1 · Public Area
INGALA / Consejo de Gobierno Booth
Present your digital TCT + passport. (TCT already paid online — this is validation only.) If somehow not pre-paid, pay $20/person cash here.
⚠️ This line is the same for everyone — it's the main bottleneck.
Step 2 · Public Area
ABG Biosecurity Scanner
Bags scanned. A plastic security seal is put on your bags.
⚠️ Do NOT remove the seal — bags must stay sealed through check-in.
Step 3 · Check-in
Avianca Priority / Business Check-in
Check sealed bags. Get boarding pass. Only after Steps 1 & 2 are done.
Step 4 · Airside
Security → Sala VIP Domestica
Clear security. Follow signs for "VIP Lounges."
